## Categories

Every team self-selects as **novice** or **main** when created. Both compete on the same ladder — the distinction determines which prize pool and finals bracket you're eligible for. The team owner can change category at any time until 48 hours before qualifiers.

## Eligibility

Each player declares their region (**UK** or **international**) and whether they are a **student** during registration. Team eligibility for finals slots is determined by member regions:

- **12 UK slots** and **4 international slots** in the main bracket finals
- International finalists receive an all-expenses-paid trip to London/Cambridge

<Warning>
  Team category and player region become **immutable 48 hours before qualifiers** to prevent gaming eligibility brackets.
</Warning>

## Prizes

**GBP 15,000** total prize pool, sponsored by Jump Trading.

Prizes are split across:
- Main bracket finals (UK + international)
- Novice tournament
- Weekly sprint tournaments

## Tournaments

### Sprint tournaments

Weekly live-streamed tournaments during the competition. Qualifiers are seeded from the current ladder standings.

### Qualifiers and finals

The competition culminates in live finals at the Cambridge Union. Top teams from the ladder qualify through the international and UK qualifiers. Finals use single-elimination brackets run on the same match infrastructure as the ladder.

## Teams

Teams consist of **1–4 players**. To compete on the ladder, you need both a team and a submitted bot.

### Creating a team

1. Log in at [game.battlecode.cam](https://game.battlecode.cam)
2. Go to **Team** in the sidebar
3. Choose a team name and select novice or main category

### Inviting members

The team owner can copy an **invite link** from the team page. Share it with teammates — they'll see a preview and join button. The invite code can be regenerated at any time.

### Managing your team

- **Owner** can remove members, rename the team, change category, and update the team bio
- **Leaving** as the sole owner transfers ownership to the next member, or deletes the team if you're the only member
- Each player can only be on **one team** at a time
