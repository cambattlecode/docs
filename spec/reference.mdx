## Cost scaling formula

$$
\text{cost} = \lfloor (1 + X) \times \text{base cost} \rfloor
$$

$$
X = \frac{\text{conv} + \text{split} + \text{armoured} + \text{bridge}}{100} + \frac{\text{road}}{200} + \frac{\text{barrier}}{100} - \frac{\text{harvester} + \text{bot} + \text{turret}}{10} - \text{foundry}
$$
