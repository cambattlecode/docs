Cambridge Battlecode is a programming competition where you write Python bots that compete in a turn-based strategy game. Your bots control autonomous mining fleets on Titan, Saturn's largest moon — harvesting resources, building infrastructure, and destroying the enemy core.

**No experience required** — beginners and veterans compete in separate brackets. Teams of 1–4, any university, any country.
